Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to all applications, games, web apps, and virtual reality games developed by huskyEstudioSoft, huskyEstudioGames, and huskyCodeAI.

Collection of Personal Data:

In our applications and games, we do not collect sensitive personal information such as names, phone numbers, contacts, email addresses, or passwords. We value and respect the privacy of our users. However, third-party services used for advertising or functionality may collect anonymous technical data (such as IP addresses or Device IDs) according to their own policies.

Windows Store Apps & Affiliate Advertising:

In our Windows-based applications, we may display affiliate advertisements from Amazon and AliExpress. These third-party platforms may use cookies or advertising identifiers to track sales attribution. You can review their privacy practices here:

  • Amazon Privacy Notice: https://www.amazon.com/privacy
  • AliExpress Privacy Policy: Legal & Privacy Center

Advertisements on Android and iOS:

Our mobile applications utilize third-party services to display advertisements. These services require an internet connection and may use tracking technologies:

  • Unity Ads: Privacy Policy
  • Chartboost: Privacy Policy
  • Applovin: Privacy Policy
  • Google Play services: Terms & Privacy

Unity 3D & Analytics:

Regarding our games and VR experiences, we use Unity Analytics to improve performance. More info at: https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy.

AI Bots & Voice Recognition:

For applications utilizing AI bots and voice commands, microphone access is used solely for processing requests. We do not store or share voice data. We utilize secure third-party APIs:

  • Microsoft Edge Web Speech / Azure: Microsoft Privacy
  • Google Speech API: Google Policies
  • Azure Speech to Text: Data Privacy
